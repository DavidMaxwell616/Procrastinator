MAX'S HANGOVER POLL

As we all know. the degree of severity a hangover takes depends not only upon the amount and types of liquor that one has drunk. but also upon one's intestinal fortitude. mental dexterity. and amount of practice with home remedies. Here are a few samples taken from interviews with a few people of renowned consumption.

"What do you take to lessen the pain of a hangover?"

"Anything mixes with Vodka." - Joan, fashionable addict "Why, whaddya got?" - Erich Kuersten, near end
"Hair of the dog! Any dog!" - Max, bitter to the end "Lots of Dope." - John Lagreco, stoned again
"Sleep and cheese." Beth, ready for more "Guns and money." - Dave Parsons, not to be outdone
