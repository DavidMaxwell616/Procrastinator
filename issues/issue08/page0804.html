BEWILDERED BY BAD VIBES

Dear Doktor,
Stupidly, I graduated college in only 4 years. With so much partying left undone, I avoided career choices and escaped to Seattle to become a gunge rocker. Well, I managed to meet one, and he managed to steal my girlfriend. In despair. I eagerly boomeranged back home to New Jersey where I still live, with my folks. My younger brother's all moved out and got girlfriends, money and cool apartments. I work part-time as a temp for a local pharmaceutical corporation, cleaning out the animal cages, disposing of the corpses. Needless to say, the smell of death is on me and the women know to keep away. How can I escape to New York, get a cool apartment and a patient girlfriend, one with bad judgment and a financially secure future.
- Guilty of Excess

Consider the lillies of the field. They toil not, and neither are they mowed. Nothing compels you to walk, and you walk not, knowing you are on the path to success. Stop. Rest. Your current path is beat. Eat at more expensive Chinese Restaurants. Consider the flesh you consume, the cages it has slept in, the lonely nights it's wept in. The misery that you devour can be redeemed to transcending love! Free the pigs! The pigs can swim! Freedom rings! The Pigs can win! The Peace Pig sings! He sings as he drinks the blood of decent farming men. He drinks with love and that's the way we never could drink him. Think about it.

TOO MANY LOSERS

Dear Doktor,
Why is it that all the attractive single men in New York don't want a girlfriend? Are they so suffused with their own sense of self worth that they no longer require a woman? Have they become, in some perverse way, their own women (I'm talking about hetaosexuals)? If so, why? And what can be done about it?
- Curiously Under No Therapy

Find a man who can only be attractive when he's around you and he'll be yours forever. Attractive men get enough favorable attention from ALL the women they meet that they don't need anyone. Ugly men get NO favorable attention from women, and need a girlfriend so desperately, they become uglier. Find a man who is in between and teach him decent grooming habits. Shine him up, and teach him to unclench his jaw. Over time the swelling will die down and he won't be too bad. For your effort, you'll have a devoted boyfriend who is scared he'll be ugly without you.
