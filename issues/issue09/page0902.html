The universe has been revealed at last and it is broke. God addressed a visibly nervous throng outside the steps of the U N. today, saying that due to massive blunders in economic thinking, the universe is inn debt over its collective ears. According to a 300.5 million year plan designed by God's personal accounting staff, each of the inhabited planets in the universe is required to pay the equivalent of 3.5 trillion dollars every other millennia. God then added that the reason our recorded civilization is under a million years old is due to the his last visit being so "disappointing" as the as earth's inhabitants obey to pay. "The universe is a dangerous place and if you want to live it, you need my protection, and I hate to break this to you, but it ain't free."
